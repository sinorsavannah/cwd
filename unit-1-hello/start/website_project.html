Overview The Crew
The USS Voyager is led by Captain Janeway whose team is stuck in the Delta Quadrant and must find their way back to the Alpha Quadrant -- a journey that could take lifetimes. Complicating matters is that a Maquis (a paramilitary terrorist organization) ship was sucked into the Delta Quadrant with Voyager. Both ships sustained heavy damages and causalties, forcing the crews to combine and work on the USS Voyager (which survived in better condition). Far from home and stuck with friends and foes alike, Janeway must beat insurmountable odds and lead her people back to Earth.
